ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВ“ГӮВ– What to Know Before You Start
ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВҸГӮВӣГғВҜГӮВёГӮВҸ ASIC Regulation
All investment products in Australia must be regulated by ASIC. Only use products with an AFS Licence. Verify at moneysmart.gov.au before investing.
ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВ’ГӮВё Tax on Investments
Capital gains are taxed at your marginal rate. Assets held 12+ months get a 50% CGT discount. Dividends may include franking credits that reduce your tax.
ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВ’ГӮВЎ Emergency Fund First
Before investing, ensure you have 3ГғВўГӮВҖГӮВ“6 months of living expenses in a high-interest savings account. As a sole trader, income can be irregular.
ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВ”ГӮВ„ Dollar-Cost Averaging
Investing the same amount regularly (weekly/monthly) reduces the impact of market volatility ГғВўГӮВҖГӮВ” you buy more when prices are low, less when high.
ГғВўГӮВңГӮВҲГғВҜГӮВёГӮВҸ Your Immigration Status
Different visas have different tax rules in Australia
ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВҮГӮВҰГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВҮГӮВә Australian Citizen or Permanent Resident
Full tax-free threshold ($18,200). Standard resident tax rates apply.
ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВ’ГӮВј Skilled Work Visa (482, 457, 186)
Resident for tax purposes if staying 183+ days. Standard rates + Medicare levy.
ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВҢГӮВҝ Working Holiday Maker (417 / 462)
Taxed at 15% on first $45,000. No tax-free threshold. No Medicare levy.
ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВҺГӮВ“ Student Visa (500)
Max 48 hrs/fortnight work. Tax-free threshold applies if main income. GST/BAS rules same.
ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВҸГӮВў Business / Investor Visa (188, 888)
Resident for tax. Foreign income may be taxable. CGT and super obligations differ.
ГғВ°ГӮВҹГӮВҢГӮВҗ Other / Bridging / Spouse Visa
Tax residency depends on stay duration. Consult a registered tax agent for your situation.
